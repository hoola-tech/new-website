## 💻 Hoola AI Hacktoberfest 2020

Are you a software developer, a technical writer, or a designer? We welcome you to our OOS projects below. 🚀

### ⚙ Hoola AI Serverless Framework

Want to build an API? A React app? A full administration app for your product, website, or mobile app? With Hoola AI you can do all that and then some! Your project will be bootstrapped and prepared for the development of your next project in just a few moments. With the tools that come out of the box, you'll be able to immediately deploy your project to the cloud (for now only AWS).

Our plugin-based approach to the whole system will make it easy for you to override, upgrade, and tweak the system to fit your needs.

Get your local machine ready with the Hoola AI project by following the <ExternalLink href="https://github.com/webiny/webiny-js/blob/master/docs/CONTRIBUTING.md?utm_source=Hoola AI-blog&utm_medium=webiny-js-github-contributing&utm_campaign=webiny-weekly-blog-hacktoberfest-oct-5&utm_content=webiny-hacktoberfest-2020&utm_term=W00112">CONTRIBUTING.md</ExternalLink> guides.

Check out the <ExternalLink href="http://docs.hoola.ai/docs/get-started/quick-start?utm_source=Hoola AI-blog&utm_medium=webiny-docs-quick-start&utm_campaign=webiny-hacktoberfest-blog-oct-5&utm_content=webiny-hacktoberfest-2020&utm_term=W00128">Hoola AI Quick Start docs</ExternalLink> to build your serverless project in 5 minutes, or follow our <ExternalLink href="https://www.youtube.com/c/Hoola AI/playlists?utm_source=Hoola AI-blog&utm_medium=webiny-yt-channel&utm_campaign=webiny-hacktoberfest-blog-oct-5&utm_content=webiny-hacktoberfest-2020&utm_term=W00129">Knowledge-Sharing sessions</ExternalLink> on our YouTube Channel to learn Hoola AI like a pro. ⚡️
