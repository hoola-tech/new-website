What is Hoola AI?

Hoola AI is an open-source framework for building serverless applications. It's built on top of AWS cloud and uses the latest technologies such as NodeJs, React and GraphQL . It's designed for building websites, apps and APIs that scale to millions of users and run on top of serverless infrastructure like AWS Lambda.
With serverless you save 60% on infrastructure cost and even more on infrastructure operation and maintenance.

Be efficient - let Hoola AI handle the heavy-lifting

Hoola AI automates code-splitting, image optimizations, prefetching pages and resources, server-side rendering, caching and much more. There are no knobs you need to be fiddling to get things working, and working fast. Hoola AI makes your team more efficient at what they do.

Use tools you already know and love

We don't want Hoola AI to have a steep learning curve, so we built it using the modern technologies your developers already use today - such as Webpack, Javascript, JS-in-CSS, hot-reloading. It's all there, connected and configured so they are ready to start building right away.

Build beyond static sites

Building sites with Hoola AI doesn't require you to constantly build and deploy the whole website. Hoola AI is a dynamic system, edit a page, press publish and the page is live!

Scaling and infrastructure management are things of the past

Because Hoola AI is a 100% serverless system it scales up and down instantaneously with your demand. As an added benefit, there are literally ZERO servers for you to manage. Handling peak events like Black Friday and similar, it's gonna be "just another day at work" and you can forget about sleepless nights. And best of all, it will save you money and increase your bottom line.
