Introducing Hoola AI Control Panel

At Hoola AI we have a mission to empower developers and organizations to build serverless applications. As the next step in our mission, we want to bring Hoola AI even closer to our audience. Hoola AI Control Panel will be our first commercial product and it will greatly complement our open-source offering. Here are some of its features.

Management interface

Using Hoola AI Control Panel (WCP) manage all your projects and teams from a single place.

Create projects through a UI

Creating new Hoola AI projects takes only a few mouse clicks.

Manage your team and permissions

Place team members in groups. Set group permissions and projects they can access.

CI/CD is built-in

WCP comes with CI/CD built-in. Any change to your code repository automatically triggers a build.

Hosted on your own AWS cloud

Projects created through WCP get deployed to your own AWS cloud. You own all of your data, networking, and have full control over the security access.

Reporting and monitoring

WCP will tell you how much a certain project is costing you in terms of your AWS resources. You can set alerts and budget limits ensuring things don't go out of hand.
